ISO 9241-110 Compliance:

Suitability for the task: The form is straightforward and focused on the login task.
Self-descriptiveness: Labels and placeholders are clear, and the form fields are appropriately labeled.
Controllability: Users can easily navigate and control the form.
Conformity with user expectations: The form follows common design patterns for login forms.
Error tolerance: Required fields help prevent submission errors.
Suitability for individualization: The form is responsive and adapts to different screen sizes.
Suitability for learning: The form is simple and easy to understand, even for new users.
